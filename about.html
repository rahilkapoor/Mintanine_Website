COMPANY BACKGROUND/OVERVIEW

We, Cuisinecraft Hospitality Private Limited has been providing Hospitality and Food solutions for almost a decade now. Waves Restaurant, Aurbindo Marg, Sarvodaya Enclave, New Delhi is our company owned running since 2011.With a capacity of 100 catering tourists from countries around the globe.

We are also planning further to expand in the industry with new products and services. Cuisinecraft Hospitality Private Limited has launched a German based Caffeinated Beverage by the name MINTANINE (MADE OUT OF THE NATURAL INGREDIENTS) as an alternative to other caffeinated/energy drinks in the market.

With lesser sugar, NO TAURINE, NO ASPERTAME and NO INOSITOL. New flavour that has no substitute currently in the Indian market. German Company YNDA GMBH, Ehingen introduced this product in 2016 starting selling in the Local as well as other German Markets.

Now Introducing in different countries around the globe like United States, China, Korea, Ukraine, Russia, Poland, Albania, South Africa and Of Course in India with us.
